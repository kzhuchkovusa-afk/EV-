### 2. Lead Created Automatically

- View all lead details

- Create tasks and reminders
- Assign leads to team members
- Track lead through sales pipeline

## üìà Lead Management Best Practices

### Quick Response Time

### Lead Qualification
Use Bitrix24 to qualify leads:
- **Hot** - Ready to book inspection immediately
- **Warm** - Interested but needs more information
- **Cold** - Just browsing, follow up later

### Follow-Up Tasks

- Send estimate within 48 hours
- Follow up after inspection
- Check-in after job completion

### Sales Pipeline

1. **New Lead** - Just submitted form
2. **Contacted** - Initial contact made
3. **Inspection Scheduled** - Appointment set
4. **Estimate Sent** - Quote provided
5. **Negotiation** - Discussing terms
6. **Won** - Contract signed
7. **Lost** - Lead didn't convert (track reason)
