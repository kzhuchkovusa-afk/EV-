Our Secret

The M-SEQUA™ Protocol

Most Other Painting Jobs

Done chaotically: the same worker patches some areas, sands others, paints something, touches up "as they go," and the client gets whatever happens — with delays, flaws, and constant need to supervise everything.

M-SEQUA™ Approach

Completely flips that approach with a proprietary protocol of technological sequence, developed specifically for Miami's climate and conditions.

Miami's high humidity, strong sun, salty air, and accelerated material aging require not just painting, but a precise, calibrated system of actions that eliminates defects and ensures a long-lasting coating.

At the core of M-SEQUA™:

A strict process map where every step is thought through, defined, and controlled. It's a clear engineering sequence that includes wall assessment, preparation, protection, application, adhesion testing, drying, final stabilization, and quality control.

Every step is performed at exactly the right time, in the right order, and checked against a checklist.

The sequence is the key to quality. If you swap even one step — the coating stops being perfect or durable.

That's why M-SEQUA™ eliminates errors at the technology level.

Four Components of the M-SEQUA™ Protocol

01

M-SEQUA™ Climate Diagnostic Segmentation

We analyze walls, humidity, temperature, and room lighting. The protocol determines which materials, primers, layers, and time intervals are needed in a specific home so the coating bonds perfectly.

Result: The coating is adapted to Miami's conditions: it doesn't fade, peel, or crack. A year later, the walls look as fresh and even as on day one — no "wrinkling," spots, or signs of moisture.

You feel calm knowing the home is protected from climate impacts and everything is under control.

02

Licensed Specialists Working Under M-SEQUA™

Each stage of painting is performed by a licensed specialist. Prep, priming, painting, quality control — every step is handled by a pro working to clear M-SEQUA™ protocols.

Result: The job is completed 2–3 times faster because there's no downtime, mistakes, or rework. In a day or two, you return to a perfectly painted home with no chaos, odor, or dust. Everything is done right the first time.

03

M-SEQUA™ Controlled Technological Sequence

A strict engineering action map: each coat is applied at the right moment, at the right speed, at the right angle, with controlled drying time and adhesion. These aren't suggestions — it's a mandatory system that does not allow mistakes.

Result: The finish goes on perfectly smooth: no stripes, no seams, no build-ups, no "orange peel." Touching such a wall, you feel smoothness like in a designer showroom — it looks good even in raking light.

04

M-SEQUA™ Final Stabilization and Quality Control

After application, the system includes checks for strength, color uniformity, adhesion, resistance to humidity and sun, and correctness of all previous steps. The team closes the project only when every line on the checklist is marked as perfect.

Result: The coating lasts many times longer: it doesn't crack, dull, "sweat," or change texture. The home stays neat, fresh, and beautiful for months and years — with no need to touch up or redo.

And over the years you only become more convinced: "I invested in quality that actually works."

M-SEQUA™ is not just a painting process. It's a controlled technological system where every step is calculated, verified, and adapted for Miami. As a result, quality becomes inevitable, and speed becomes a natural consequence of a precise sequence.

The Process

How the "Turnkey Painting" Service Works

1

Assessment + Plan

Professional Inspection

Before we start, we precisely determine the condition of your walls and what needs to be done so the paint lasts for years. You get an estimate and understand what you're paying for.

Fixed Price

Everything is written into the contract. If we discover additional work during the process, we agree the cost with you before we start it, not after. No surprise add-ons.

Convenient Schedule

You choose: we paint room by room (while you live in the home), or everything at once while you're away. We align the schedule to your life, not the other way around.

2

Preparation

Full Protection of Your Property

All furniture is wrapped in plastic and moved. Floors are fully covered. Chandeliers, outlets, artwork — everything is masked. When we leave, there isn't a single drop of paint where it shouldn't be.

Professional Wall Preparation

We remove old paint, fill cracks and holes, sand unevenness, and apply the right primer for Miami's humid climate. This stage is half the success. We don't cut corners here.

Zero Dust in the Home

Every sander is connected to an industrial vacuum. Doorways are sealed. At the end of each day we clean: tools are put away, the home is tidy. You won't spend a week scrubbing white dust off your chandelier.

The Rest of the Home Stays Untouched

Dust and odors don't spread to neighboring rooms. The work stays in one area; life continues in the rest of the home.

3

Painting

Organized Team of Licensed Specialists

Each stage is handled by a pro: prep → priming → painting → quality check. No downtime, no confusion. In a day or two we paint 1–2 full rooms or more, depending on the scope.

Proven Materials Selected for Miami

Sherwin-Williams or Benjamin Moore. For bathrooms — anti-mildew paints (so mold doesn't come back). For sunny rooms — paints with UV protection (so the color doesn't fade). Every material is selected specifically for homes in Miami.

Ideal Application Technique

On large flat surfaces we use sprayers (no stripes or build-ups). In corners and details — hand work with professional brushes (no drips). The result looks like a design magazine.

Safe Paints

Low-VOC formulas with minimal odor. A couple of hours after painting, you can be in the room without discomfort. No suffocating smell for days.

Daily Order

Every evening tools are put away and the space is ventilated. You come home and see clean progress, not construction chaos.

4

Final Check + Warranty

You approve, we take responsibility

You Inspect the Result First

Take blue painter's tape, walk through the home, and mark any detail you don't like. Wrong color? Smudge? Unevenness? We fix it within 24 hours at our expense.

Deep Cleaning

All painted rooms are brought to a perfect state. You see your updated home at its best, without dust or dirt.

Payment Only After Your Approval

You sign the acceptance form and only then pay. If something doesn't suit you, don't pay until we fix it.
